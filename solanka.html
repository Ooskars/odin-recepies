Solanka

Description:

Although there are no classic ingredients such as kidneys and fresh beef and pork, the soup also gets its full flavor with dried meat. Simple enough, very filling and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Meat (smoked, dried ham, salami, dried sausage)
  • Carrots 2 pcs
  • Small onion
  • Small lemon
  • Black olives
  • Marinated cucumbers
  • Potatos
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into strips, grate the carrots (on a coarse grater), finely chop the onion, cut the cucumbers and cut the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Fry the onions on the pan, after a short time (1 - 2 min.) add the meat and fry for a few minutes, then add the grated carrots and cucumbers, fry until the carrots are a little golden (2 - 4 min.), then add the tomato sauce and saute a little more.
  3. Pour warm water into the pot (amount depends on the amount of ingredients and the number of portions. 2 pers. about 1 l.) and pour in everything that is on the pan, mix thoroughly and boil, and add cold water (as much liquid soup as you want). After about 1 min. add potatoes and cook until soft. If you want, you can add salt to taste.
  4. When the soup is ready - serve. Add a slice of lemon, olives.
