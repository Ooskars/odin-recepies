Banana bread

Description:

Dont know what to do with all the bananas you bought three days ago? Don't worry! Banana bread is easy recepie that will make use of all of your bananas and make a great sweet to enjoy with a cup of milk, tea or coffee.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 tbsp vannilla extract
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp ground cinammon
  • 75g melted butter
  • 1 scrambled egg
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 165g flour

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 180 degrees celsius
  2. Peel bananas and smash them
  3. Add butter and stir
  4. Add sugar, egg, vannilla and other spices
  5. Stir good
  6. Add baking soda and salt
  7. Stir good
  8. Gradually add flour while stiring
  9. Butter the baking tray and pour inside liquid dough
  10. Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until first bubble appears out of loaf of bread
  11. Cool down the baking tray before taking out the loaf of bread
  12. Enjoy!
