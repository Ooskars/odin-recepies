Chicken with vegetables "Asian" style

Description:

Vegetables, chicken, honey and soja sauce - perfect combination that excites, is quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillet
  • Honey
  • Soya sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into strips, fry in a pan with olive oil, add 2 tablespoons of honey, sesame seeds, then 2 tablespoons of soy sauce. Take it out of the pan.
  2. In the same pan, fry carrots, mushrooms, peppers, broccoli and zucchini, add a little salt, pepper, soy sauce.
  3. Return the chicken to the vegetables. Simmer for up to 10 minutes. I really like it when the vegetables are semi-soft. I boiled the pasta and put it in the pan. I recommend you try it!
